Welcome to New Mountania Resort

New Mountania Resort, Masinagudi offers a panoramic breathtaking view of the magnificent surroundings. The Resort is located in a commanding position in the heart of Masinagudi. Our resort covers an area of 6.5 acres. Look out of your bedroom and you'll be presented with a perpetually changing view of the environment. One moment it's misty, the other it's shrouded in shadows. Then when you least expect it, it's jaw-droppingly spectacular. While soaking in the view is an activity in itself, you'll see that the list of things to do is limited only by the time you have. It isn't uncommon to hear the calls of animals and the trumpeting of elephants as they pass by the resort.

The resort is blessed with the masterpiece of Nature, where each flower is a soul blossoming out to pleasant ambience. New Mountania Resort consists of aesthetically appointed cottages with modern facilities, kids play area, outdoor and indoor games. The specialty of New Mountania Resorts is that it caters vegetarian and non-vegetarian delicacies of various types. The ambience is just right for a relaxed and an adventurous holiday.

A few facilities provided by the resort include restaurant, pick up facility and recreational activities. Residents are offered safe and reliable pick up facility for convenient commute. For a culinary feast, one can relish a wide variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian cuisines prepared by the cook with finesse. These cuisines are visually appetizing and offer a burst of delicious flavour. Tourists can also indulge into an interesting array of recreational activities like safari, trekking, kids play area, indoor and outdoor games.

About Masinagudi

Masinagudi is an exotic locale situated a few kilometers before Ooty, on the Karnataka - Tamil Nadu Border. Masinagudi is a beautiful place on the foothills of Nilgiris just after the Bandipur Wildlife Sanctuary in Karnataka.

The place is lush green, peaceful and adventurous at the same time with a lot of trekking and hiking options available for one and all.

Masinagudi is at a distance of 250 Km from Bangalore. The road leading to Masinagudi is beautiful with lot of sunflower fields on both the sides. Enjoy a peaceful relaxing vacation with beautiful wildlife and sightseeing.

It is a fascinating tourist destination. This is one of the most beautiful places in India. Masinagudi is at the heart of the Mudumalai forest reserve. It offers spectacular views and temperatures between 14 to 23 degree celcius.
