MENU

Idli

Idli are a type of savoury rice cake, popular as breakfast foods in southern India and northern Sri Lanka.

Poori

Poori is an unleavened deep-fried bread. It is eaten for breakfast or as a snack or light meal.

Masala Dosa

Dosa is a thin crispy crepe made using fermented rice and lentil batter. It is one that has spiced and seasoned potatoes stuffed in it.

Skewer

Skewers are used while grilling or roasting meats and fish, and in other culinary applications.

Rice Bowl

It consists of fish, meat, vegetables or other ingredients simmered together and served over rice.

Biryani

Long-grained rice flavored with exotic spices, such as saffron, is layered with lamb, chicken, fish, or vegetables, and a thick gravy.

Dhal Makhani

A punjabi staple dish, Dal Makhani is the absolute favorite recipe that can easily be cooked to perfection.

Dhal Tadka

A mix of moong dal and arhar dal, this is a flavourful lentil preparation from Punjab.
